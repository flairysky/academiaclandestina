About

Academia Clandestina exists to help students hack their way through university life. Created by students, for students, we deliver (legal) hacks, tips, tricks, and guides — cutting through the noise to give you only the information you actually need. That’s why our content is written the way it is: short, sharp, and to the point. No bullshit. No boring lectures. Just what you need to survive and thrive.
