About

Academia Clandestina exists to help students hack their way through university life.

Created by students, for students, we deliver (legal) hacks, tips, tricks, and guides — cutting through the noise to give you only the information you actually need.
That’s why our content is written the way it is: short, sharp, and to the point.
No bullshit. No boring lectures. Just what you need to survive and thrive.

P.S. We do not guarantee that any of the content we provide actually works.

Interessted? We are students based at ETHZ. If you are an experienced backend engineer and would like to join, write to us on Instagram (working on other stuff rather than setting up email) and tell us why we should let you get involved (a green carpet-looking GitHub log wouldn't hurt).
